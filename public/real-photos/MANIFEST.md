# Real Photos Manifest

Every file here is real, sourced from Price-Less Building Center's own
public Instagram, Facebook, Google Business listing, or a downloaded
Facebook video. Zero stock. Zero invention.

When choosing an asset for a page, look here first. Use a real asset if
one fits; only fall back to `/test-images/` or `/catalog-images/` (AI
generated) for product hero shots where we don't have a real photo.

## 🎯 BRAND MARKS (LOGOS)

These are the canonical brand identities. Use them anywhere the brand
name appears as a display element.

### `logo-official.webp` (+ `@2x`) — THE OFFICIAL LOGO
**320 × 320 px, square circle on transparent-ish background.** Pulled
straight from the Price-Less Building Center Instagram profile picture.
A perfect circular badge:
- Outer ring text: "PRICE-LESS BUILDING CENTER" (top arc), "BUILDERS CORNER CABINETRY & DESIGN" (bottom arc)
- Center: a green "tree of tools" graphic (tree silhouette made out of hammers, saws, pulls, hardware) with a black house silhouette and green roof at the base
- This is the SINGLE OFFICIAL MARK for the combined business.
**Use it:** as the BrandLogo for both brands when a clean badge is needed; in the site-footer sister-brands column; as a favicon source.

### `sign-logo.webp` (+ `@2x`)
**Crop of the actual hand-painted "PRICE-LESS BUILDING CENTER" wood sign mounted on the storefront.** Three stacked lines in warm-orange western serif on weathered white plank. The real, in-the-wild Price-Less wordmark.
**Use it:** as the wordmark in the Price-Less site-header (currently in use). Big and unmistakable.

### `seal-full.webp` (+ `@2x`)
**Cleaner crop of the same circular badge as `logo-official.webp`**, pulled from the WAOW grocery-giveaway promo graphic. Slightly desaturated background (grocery-store backdrop bleed-through). 210 × 210.
**Use it:** secondary placement only. Prefer `logo-official.webp` (cleaner background).
